Preseason preview Tuesday, October 08, 2024 — 22:30 UTC

Brooklyn Nets at Los Angeles Clippers

Frontwave Arena · Oceanside, CA · Preseason opener

The Nets bring their wing-driven identity to the California coast while the Clippers spotlight bench playmakers in a new arena.

Why it matters in October

Brooklyn is using this trip to gauge how Mikal Bridges handles increased usage alongside Dennis Schröder. Cam Thomas will see heavy scoring minutes while Noah Clowney and Day’Ron Sharpe battle for rotation leverage.

The Clippers are protecting the core veterans but want to see Terance Mann and Bones Hyland push pace. Norman Powell’s downhill attacks and Moussa Diabaté’s rim presence are focal points as Tyronn Lue maps second-unit spacing.

Defensive communication is the swing factor. Brooklyn’s staff wants sharper shell rotations after last year’s slippage, while the Clippers are stressing defensive rebounding to fuel early offense in Oceanside’s smaller venue.

  • Does Bridges create efficiently when the Clippers send length at him in early traps?
  • Can the Nets keep Los Angeles off the glass when Zubac sits and Diabaté anchors the middle?
  • How do the Clippers’ pace pushes look with Powell and Hyland sharing the backcourt?
