Preseason preview Friday, October 11, 2024 — 22:30 UTC

Portland Trail Blazers at Los Angeles Clippers

Climate Pledge Arena · Seattle, WA · Preseason opener

Seattle’s preseason stage features Portland’s youth movement against a Clippers core intent on refining star synergy.

Why it matters in October

Portland wants Scoot Henderson to run the show with Anfernee Simons spotting up more often. Shaedon Sharpe and Jabari Walker will test lineup versatility while Duop Reath stretches opposing bigs beyond the arc.

The Clippers expect to play their stars in short bursts, prioritizing new Harden-Leonard actions and Paul George’s off-ball movement. Kobe Brown and Ivica Zubac provide stability while Los Angeles measures conditioning.

Both sides are eyeing defensive habits. Portland needs better containment at the point of attack, while the Clippers are monitoring pick-and-roll coverage after adding more switchable forwards.

  • Can Scoot Henderson control pace without being baited into turnovers by Los Angeles’ veterans?
  • How sharp do Harden and Leonard look running two-man actions after limited offseason reps together?
  • Does Portland’s young frontcourt hold up on the glass against Zubac and the Clippers’ size?
