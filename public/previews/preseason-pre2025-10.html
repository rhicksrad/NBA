Los Angeles Lakers at Golden State Warriors

Chase Center · San Francisco, CA

Game essentials

Los Angeles Lakers test guards Adou Thiero, Augustas Marčiulionis, and Austin Reaves as camp reps ramp up. Golden State Warriors spotlight guards Brandin Podziemski, Buddy Hield, and Jimmy Butler while tracking chemistry.

Matchup snapshot

  • Los Angeles Lakers vs. Golden State Warriors
  • Backcourt spotlight: guards Adou Thiero, Augustas Marčiulionis, and Austin Reaves vs. guards Brandin Podziemski, Buddy Hield, and Jimmy Butler.
  • Frontcourt reps: bigs Christian Koloko, Deandre Ayton, and Jaxson Hayes vs. bigs Marques Bolden and Quinten Post.

Narrative questions to watch

Los Angeles Lakers bring their guards Adou Thiero, Augustas Marčiulionis, and Austin Reaves, their wings Anton Watson, Dalton Knecht, and Eric Dixon, and their bigs Christian Koloko and Deandre Ayton into camp.

Golden State Warriors bring their guards Brandin Podziemski, Buddy Hield, and Jimmy Butler, their wings Alex Toohey, Blake Hinson, and Draymond Green, and their bigs Marques Bolden and Quinten Post into camp.

    Golden State Warriors counter with guards Brandin Podziemski, Buddy Hield, and Jimmy Butler to steady tempo.

    Los Angeles Lakers lean on bigs Christian Koloko, Deandre Ayton, and Jaxson Hayes to control the interior rhythms.

    Golden State Warriors expect bigs Marques Bolden and Quinten Post to anchor their paint touches.

    Los Angeles Lakers track reserve pushes from Anton Watson, Christian Koloko, and Dalton Knecht as camp competition heats up.
