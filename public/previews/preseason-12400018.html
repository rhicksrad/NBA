Preseason preview Tuesday, October 08, 2024 — 19:30 UTC

Indiana Pacers at Atlanta Hawks

State Farm Arena · Atlanta, GA · Preseason opener

Indiana’s high-octane offense meets an Atlanta roster reshaped around top pick Zaccharie Risacher.

Why it matters in October

The Pacers want to maintain last season’s league-leading pace while tightening their defense. Head coach Rick Carlisle will stagger Tyrese Haliburton and Bennedict Mathurin early, then feature Andrew Nembhard, Ben Sheppard, and second-year forward Jarace Walker to assess their growth. Newly signed forward Obi Toppin is also on a workload plan after knee soreness in August.

Atlanta, meanwhile, is introducing Risacher and monitoring how he fits alongside Trae Young and Jalen Johnson. Quin Snyder is testing bigger lineups with Onyeka Okongwu at the four and free-agent addition De'Andre Hunter sliding to guard larger wings. Kobe Bufkin’s ball-handling and AJ Griffin’s health are under the microscope after inconsistent sophomore seasons.

Clint Capela remains on a maintenance program but is expected to play short bursts. Both teams are otherwise healthy and emphasizing defensive communication and transition efficiency.

  • Can Risacher hold up defensively against Indiana’s constant motion?
  • Does Walker show improved decision-making as a small-ball five?
  • Which bench guard — Nembhard or Bufkin — controls tempo better?
