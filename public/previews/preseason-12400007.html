Preseason preview Sunday, October 06, 2024 — 17:00 UTC

Washington Wizards at Toronto Raptors

Scotiabank Arena · Toronto, ON · Preseason opener

A rebuilding Wizards roster debuts top pick Alex Sarr while the Raptors evaluate their revamped backcourt depth.

Why it matters in October

Washington’s new front office wants to see the Alex Sarr–Bilal Coulibaly pairing immediately. Expect Sarr to split time between the five and a roaming weak-side role, while second-year guard Ryan Rollins and rookie Bub Carrington manage creation duty with Tyus Jones on a minutes limit.

Toronto has continuity but different priorities: Darko Rajaković is staggering Scottie Barnes and RJ Barrett while gauging how Immanuel Quickley and rookie Jamal Shead run the offense. Jakob Poeltl is still ramping from postseason ankle soreness, opening the door for rookie forward Jonathan Mogbo to soak up minutes.

Both clubs are healthy outside of Poeltl’s restriction. The Raptors are focusing on defensive pressure and tempo, and the Wizards want to test jumbo lineups that can speed up their rebuild.

  • How comfortable does Sarr look anchoring drop coverage against Toronto’s drive-heavy wings?
  • Can Quickley balance his scoring with table-setting for Barrett and Barnes?
  • Which Washington guard seizes backup minutes while Tyus Jones eases in?
