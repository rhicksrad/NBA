Preseason preview Saturday, October 05, 2024 — 19:00 UTC

Golden State Warriors at Los Angeles Clippers

Stan Sheriff Center · Honolulu, HI · Preseason opener

Both Pacific powers are in evaluation mode in Honolulu, sharpening bench identity and new combinations around veteran cores.

Why it matters in October

Golden State has promised extended usage for Jonathan Kuminga and Moses Moody, with the staff eager to see how rookie big Quinten Post can stretch the floor alongside Draymond Green. Chris Paul and Stephen Curry will log short stints before handing the keys to Brandin Podziemski and the hybrid bench units.

Los Angeles wants to integrate new faces around Kawhi Leonard and Paul George without overtaxing either star. James Harden’s pick-and-roll reps with rookie big Jordan Miller and second-year forward Kobe Brown are a focal point, while Russell Westbrook is likely to sit after a heavy summer workload.

Tyronn Lue has emphasised defensive communication, so expect to see Amir Coffey and Bones Hyland pressed into switch-heavy looks. For the Warriors, the staff continues to test bigger lineups that can survive non-Curry minutes.

  • Can Kuminga sustain the playmaking progress he flashed late last season?
  • Which Clippers reserve wing steps forward with Terance Mann on a minutes cap?
  • Does Quinten Post’s spacing convince Steve Kerr to pair him with Trayce Jackson-Davis?
