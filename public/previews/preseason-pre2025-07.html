Minnesota Timberwolves at Denver Nuggets

Game essentials

Minnesota Timberwolves test guards Anthony Edwards, Donte DiVincenzo, and Jaylen Clark as camp reps ramp up. Denver Nuggets spotlight guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones while tracking chemistry.

Matchup snapshot

  • Minnesota Timberwolves vs. Denver Nuggets
  • Backcourt spotlight: guards Anthony Edwards, Donte DiVincenzo, and Jaylen Clark vs. guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones.
  • Frontcourt reps: bigs Naz Reid, Rocco Zikarsky, and Rudy Gobert vs. bigs Jonas Valanciunas, Moses Brown, and Nikola Jokic.

Narrative questions to watch

Minnesota Timberwolves bring their guards Anthony Edwards, Donte DiVincenzo, and Jaylen Clark, their wings Alize Johnson, Enrique Freeman, and Jaden McDaniels, and their bigs Naz Reid and Rocco Zikarsky into camp.

Denver Nuggets bring their guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones, their wings Aaron Gordon, Cameron Johnson, and DaRon Holmes II, and their bigs Jonas Valanciunas and Moses Brown into camp.

    Minnesota Timberwolves will test guards Anthony Edwards, Donte DiVincenzo, and Jaylen Clark to sort ball-handling combinations.

    Denver Nuggets counter with guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones to steady tempo.

  2. Frontcourt combinations

    Minnesota Timberwolves lean on bigs Naz Reid, Rocco Zikarsky, and Rudy Gobert to control the interior rhythms.

    Denver Nuggets expect bigs Jonas Valanciunas, Moses Brown, and Nikola Jokic to anchor their paint touches.

  3. Depth auditions

    Minnesota Timberwolves track reserve pushes from Alize Johnson, Enrique Freeman, and Jaden McDaniels as camp competition heats up.

    Denver Nuggets gauge whether Aaron Gordon, Cameron Johnson, and DaRon Holmes II can carve out rotation trust.

    • Key reserves — Denver Nuggets: Aaron Gordon, Cameron Johnson, and DaRon Holmes II
