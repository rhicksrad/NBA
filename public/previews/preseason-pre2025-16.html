Denver Nuggets at Toronto Raptors

Game essentials

Denver Nuggets test guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones as camp reps ramp up. Toronto Raptors spotlight guards AJ Lawson, Alijah Martin, and Chucky Hepburn while tracking chemistry.

Matchup snapshot

  • Denver Nuggets vs. Toronto Raptors
  • Backcourt spotlight: guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones vs. guards AJ Lawson, Alijah Martin, and Chucky Hepburn.
  • Frontcourt reps: bigs Jonas Valanciunas, Moses Brown, and Nikola Jokic vs. bigs Jakob Poeltl, Olivier Sarr, and Ulrich Chomche.

Narrative questions to watch

Denver Nuggets bring their guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones, their wings Aaron Gordon, Cameron Johnson, and DaRon Holmes II, and their bigs Jonas Valanciunas and Moses Brown into camp.

Toronto Raptors bring their guards AJ Lawson, Alijah Martin, and Chucky Hepburn, their wings Brandon Ingram, Collin Murray-Boyles, and David Roddy, and their bigs Jakob Poeltl and Olivier Sarr into camp.

  1. Backcourt auditions

    Denver Nuggets will test guards Bruce Brown, Christian Braun, and Curtis Jones to sort ball-handling combinations.

    Toronto Raptors counter with guards AJ Lawson, Alijah Martin, and Chucky Hepburn to steady tempo.

  2. Frontcourt combinations

    Denver Nuggets lean on bigs Jonas Valanciunas, Moses Brown, and Nikola Jokic to control the interior rhythms.

    Toronto Raptors expect bigs Jakob Poeltl, Olivier Sarr, and Ulrich Chomche to anchor their paint touches.

  3. Depth auditions

    Denver Nuggets track reserve pushes from Aaron Gordon, Cameron Johnson, and DaRon Holmes II as camp competition heats up.

    Toronto Raptors gauge whether Brandon Ingram, Collin Murray-Boyles, and David Roddy can carve out rotation trust.

    • Key reserves — Toronto Raptors: Brandon Ingram, Collin Murray-Boyles, and David Roddy
