Game essentials

Detroit Pistons test guards Aaron Estrada, Ausar Thompson, and Brice Williams as camp reps ramp up. Matchup snapshot

  • Detroit Pistons vs. Memphis Grizzlies

  • Frontcourt reps: bigs Charles Bediako, Isaiah Stewart, and Jalen Duren vs. bigs Armando Bacot, Jaren Jackson Jr., and Jock Landale.
