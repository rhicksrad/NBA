Golden State Warriors at Sacramento Kings

Golden 1 Center · Sacramento, CA · Preseason opener

The Northern California rivalry previews playoff-level pace as both teams sharpen offensive timing for the fall.

Why it matters in October

Golden State wants early looks at Jonathan Kuminga as a featured scorer beside Stephen Curry. Expect Brandin Podziemski to initiate second units while Trayce Jackson-Davis anchors screening actions.

Sacramento counters with De’Aaron Fox and Domantas Sabonis running familiar dribble-handoff actions, but Keegan Murray will experiment with more on-ball touches. Malik Monk’s bench spark remains a priority as the Kings monitor defensive growth.

Both staffs have circled defensive rebounding and transition control. The Warriors aim to keep the Kings out of early offense, while Sacramento wants to pressure Golden State’s bench bigs into foul trouble.

  • How sharp does Golden State’s motion look with Curry and Kuminga sharing initiator reps?
  • Does Keegan Murray’s expanded role produce efficient shots against Golden State’s switching?
  • Can the Kings limit second-chance looks when Trayce Jackson-Davis and Kevon Looney patrol the glass?
